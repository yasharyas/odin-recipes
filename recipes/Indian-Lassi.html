Indian Lassi

Ingredients

  • ice cubes
  • 1 ¾ cups plain yogurt
  • 1 ½ cups ice water
  • 6 cubes ice, crushed
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 pinch salt

Directions

  1. Fill 6 tall glasses with ice cubes. Place yogurt, ice water, crushed ice, sugar, and salt in a blender; blend until frothy. Pour over ice cubes in the glasses to serve

Tips

You can substitute 1/2 teaspoon salt for the sugar for a savory lassi.

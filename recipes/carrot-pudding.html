Gajar Ka Halwa (Carrot Halwa)

Carrot halwa, or gajar ka halwa, is a traditional Indian dessert that's deliciously rich and creamy. This recipe uses whole milk, not condensed milk. Serve warm with vanilla ice cream for a real treat!

Ingredients

  1. 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  2. 2 pounds carrots, grated
  3. 1 ½ quarts whole milk
  4. 2 cups white sugar
  5. ½ cup raisins or sultanas
  6. 1 ½ teaspoons ground cardamom
  7. 1 pinch saffron threads
  8. 8 tablespoons chopped pistachio nuts
